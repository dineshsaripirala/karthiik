Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Trust

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Aryan Kapoor, Stackly began as a single-specialty clinic with a vision: to make world-class healthcare accessible to every Indian family. Today, we operate 12 hospitals across 8 cities.

Our team of 340+ specialists, 1,200+ nurses, and 2,000+ support staff work as one unified force — guided by the belief that every patient deserves not just treatment, but healing.
