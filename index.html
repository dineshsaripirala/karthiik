FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

How do I book an appointment? ▼
You can book through our website, mobile app, or by calling our helpline. Most appointments can be confirmed within 24 hours.
Do you accept health insurance? ▼
Yes, we accept all major health insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, and government schemes like Ayushman Bharat.
What are your OPD timings? ▼
OPD timings are Mon–Sat 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM and Sunday 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.
How soon are lab results available? ▼
Routine tests are ready within 24–48 hours. Critical and emergency tests are available within 2–4 hours. Results are shared digitally.
Is telemedicine available? ▼
Yes, we offer video consultations with our specialists. You can book teleconsultations through the patient dashboard after signing in.
